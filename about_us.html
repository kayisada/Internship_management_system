About Us

SAN TECH LTD is a private company created since 2019 and based in Kigali, Rwanda.

SAN TECH was founded by young Rwandan Software Engineers to contribute to the national and regional IT sector, driving technology evolution towards next level in Rwanda and abroad. We sharpening skills of Rwandan to use technology in thinking about invention and innovation in product development and virtual reality.

As product, we offer E-Visitors which is a system that helps in collecting attendance and movements of visitors, staffs, employees and equipment monitoring both personal and institution. In other words e-visitors is an automated system in data collection.

As services, we offer graphic design, electronic maintenance, networking and artificial intelligence development. We offer trainings, internships, IT Consultancy, as well as IT Innovative solutions related product and Tech hub development.
